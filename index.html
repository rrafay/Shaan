Our Story

Shan Builders & Co. possesses a wealth of experience in various areas, including mixed-use developments, new construction, refurbishment, design and build projects, and traditional forms of contracting.

This extensive experience, combined with our highly knowledgeable and experienced staff, allows us to approach projects in an exceptionally professional and competitive manner. The skill sets required to successfully deliver high-end residential projects and commercial shell and core buildings are distinct, and Shan Builders & Co. has consistently demonstrated the ability to excel in both areas on numerous high-profile projects.

Successful project delivery stems from a highly focused approach. We ensure the active involvement of our main board directors in every project, from inception to completion, as we limit our active projects to a maximum of 10-12 at a time. This approach ensures that any issues are addressed efficiently and promptly, and it allows for robust quality control measures.

At Shan Builders & Co., we maintain our own fleet of plant and equipment, enabling us to compete aggressively in terms of pricing. Our primary goal is to become our clients' preferred choice for construction-related services. Our customers and partners view us as integral to their success. We proactively anticipate their needs and consistently deliver on every commitment we make.

Our people, our unwavering focus, and our proven track record for successful project delivery make a compelling case for selecting Shan Builders & Co. as your preferred construction partner.
