About Us

Shan Builders & Co has a wealth of experience in mixed use like developments, new build and refurbishment, in design and build and traditional forms of contracting. This experience combined with highly knowledgeable and experienced staff provides an extremely professional and competitive approach. The skills sets required to deliver high end residential projects and to deliver commercials shell and core buildings are quite different and Shan Builders & Co have repeatedly shown the ability to deliver both for clients on many high profile projects. Successful project delivery stems from much focused approach. Main board director involvement is ensured on every project from start to finish as only 10-12 projects maximum are run at a time, ensuring that issues are dealt with efficiently and quickly and there is strong quality control. At Shan Builders & Co we have our own fleet of plant and equipment which gives the ability to compete very aggressively in terms of our pricing structure. Our aim is to be our clients' first choice for construction related services. Customers and partners see us as integral to their success. We will anticipate their needs and deliver on every commitment we make. Our people, our focus and our track record for delivery present a very strong case for choosing Shan Builders & Co as the preferred construction partner.
